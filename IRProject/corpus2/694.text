A Comparison of Disks and TapesThe principal characteristics of current magnetic disks and tape units are summarized and compared. Some of the characteristics of disk files are illustrated in a sorting example and compared to a tapesort. The conclusion is presented that disk files are competitive to tapes in some important applications.CACM October, 1963Hess, H.CA631031 JB March 13, 1978  4:21 PM
