Compact Representation of Contour Plots for Phone Line TransmissionMethods for the compact representation of contour plots are described and tested.  These are intended to reduce the cost of transmitting contour plots over phone lines.  We feel some of these methods could be used to transmit contour plots over voice grade phone lines.CACM June, 1974Adams, J.
