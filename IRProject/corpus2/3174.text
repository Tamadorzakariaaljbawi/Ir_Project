Password Security: A Case HistoryThis paper describes the history of the design of the password security scheme on a remotely accessed time-sharing system. The present design was the result of countering observed attempts to penetrate the system.  The result is a compromise between extreme security and ease of use.CACM November, 1979Morris, R.
