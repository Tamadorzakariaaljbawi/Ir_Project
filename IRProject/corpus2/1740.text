An Inexpensive Braille Terminal DeviceThe active use of time-shared facilities for blind programmers requires a braille terminal system.  Details are given for the construction of a brailler from a model 33 teletype by modifying the print head and increasing the resiliency of the platen. A description of the programming needed to drive the brailler is presented.CACM June, 1968Anderson, G. B. Rogers, D.
