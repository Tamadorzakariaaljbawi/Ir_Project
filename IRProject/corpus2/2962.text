Production and Employment of Ph.D.'s in Computer Science-1976Statistics are presented on the production and employment of Ph.D.'s in computer science for the calendar year 1975-76.  Data include profiles of graduate students and of faculty at 60 Ph.D.-producing departments as well as a breakdown of degrees granted by specialty areas.  Significant trends are noted and comparisons with comparable data gathered for the 1974-75 calendar year are made.CACM June, 1977Taulbee, O. E. Conte, S.
