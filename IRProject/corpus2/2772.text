On Maintenance of the Opportunity List for Class-Teacher Timetable ProblemsOne of the principal components of procedures for the solution of class-teacher timetable problems is that for maintenance of the opportunity list.  Opportunity list maintenance methods are based on necessary conditions for the existence of a solution.
